About Me

As someone who has always been up for a new adventure, pursuing a career in web development is one of the most exciting and challenging adventures yet. I am thrilled to join an industry that is creating new ways to share information and connect people. Having worked in various hospitality positions and even a few years as an English teacher in South Korea and Spain, I value the way the internet has transformed our world. I hope to use the ever-expanding skills learned through the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Minnesota to pursue a career that allows me to tackle new problems and lets my creativity shine.

In my free time I enjoy listening to music, visiting used book stores, and traveling. In 2017, I spent time in Asia, South America, and Europe.
